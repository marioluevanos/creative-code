Spacing

Spacing increments are based on an 8px grid, but we do allow for 4px or 2x smaller increments when needed, most likely in inner grids (that is the spacing and padding inside a component).

For stacking elements and modules refer to stacking or slotted components. Spacing can vary per stylistic-design needs, so you can use a 2px increment to get the right vertical spacing in your components.
